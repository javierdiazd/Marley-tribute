Bob Marley

The Best Exponent of Reggae



February 6 of 1945 – May 11 of 1981

✝


Was a Jamaican singer, songwriter and musician. Considered one of the pioneers

of reggae, his musical career was marked by fusing elements of reggae, ska, and

rocksteady, as well as his distinctive vocal and songwriting style. Marley's

contributions to music increased the visibility of Jamaican music worldwide, and

made him a global figure in popular culture for over a decade. Read more...
